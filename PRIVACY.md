# Privacy

Ask AI runs entirely on your device using Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano model.

- Your question and the page content are processed locally. They are never sent to any server.
- No accounts, no sign-in, no usage data, no analytics, no tracking.
- The only thing stored on your device is the local model itself (downloaded once by Chrome) and your most recent question (so the popup remembers it if you reopen).

If that ever stops being true, this file will say so.
