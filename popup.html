On-device AI not available

This extension uses Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano model. It needs:

  • Chrome 138 or newer
  • Windows 10/11 or macOS 13+
  • 4 GB+ GPU VRAM or 16 GB RAM
  • 22 GB free storage for the model
  • Unmetered internet for the first download

You may also need to enable the flag at chrome://flags/#prompt-api-for-gemini-nano and set it to "Enabled".

How it works
100% on-device

This extension uses Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano model, which runs entirely on your computer. Your question and the page content are processed locally — they never leave your browser.

What that means for you
  • No account or sign-in
  • Your question and page are never sent to any server
  • No usage data, analytics, or tracking
  • Free. No subscription, no paywall.
Works on YouTube too

On a YouTube video page, Ask AI uses the video transcript instead of the page text. Captions need to be available — look for the CC button on the player.

About Gemini Nano

Gemini Nano is Google's on-device AI model, built directly into Chrome. It's part of Chrome itself — not something this extension installs or controls. All processing happens on your device using your CPU or GPU.
